Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026

COC Songs ("we", "our", or "the app") is a free hymnal application for the Church of Christ community providing Telugu and English songs for worship.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app operates entirely offline on your device.

Data Stored Locally

The app stores the following data only on your device using your browser's local storage:

  • Favorite songs — your selected favorite song numbers
  • Theme preference — light or dark mode setting
  • Font size preference — your chosen lyrics font size

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party services, analytics, advertising, or tracking tools.

Internet Access

The app works fully offline. No internet connection is required after installation. No data is sent to any server.

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. It is safe for users of all ages.

Permissions

The app does not require any special device permissions such as camera, microphone, location, or contacts.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
